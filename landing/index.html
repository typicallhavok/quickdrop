Everything you need to move files. Nothing you don't.

A focused tool that does one job well — fast, secure transfers between the devices you already own.

End-to-end encryption

Every transfer is encrypted with AES. Devices verify each other's identity, and you choose which ones to trust.

Direct, no cloud

Files go straight from one device to the other over your local network. Nothing is uploaded, stored, or seen by anyone else.

Resume interrupted transfers

Lost connection or closed the app? Pick up exactly where you left off instead of starting the whole file again.

Clipboard sharing

Send the text on your clipboard to another device with one tap — links, codes, and snippets land instantly.

Drag, drop, done

Drop files onto the window or share straight from Android. Multiple files queue up and send together.

Automatic discovery

Nearby devices show up on their own. No IP addresses, no pairing codes, no setup to remember.

Performance

Built to use your full link speed.

Because transfers stay on your own network and skip the internet round-trip, Quickdrop moves at the speed of your hardware — not your upload plan. The transfer engine is written in Rust, so it stays fast and memory-safe with no garbage-collection pauses. Tuned socket buffers and large streaming chunks keep the connection saturated from start to finish.

  • Native engine written in Rust — no runtime overhead, no GC pauses
  • Streams in large chunks with a parallel reader/writer pipeline
  • Enlarged TCP windows so throughput tracks the link rate
  • No compression detours or cloud hops between devices
